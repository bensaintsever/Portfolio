METHOD

What makes a project isn't the final screen. It's the decision we dared to make.

01 · FRAME

The problem

Understand the field before the screen: who decides, with what data, under what constraints.

02 · DO

What I did

Explore several directions, prototype fast, put it in front of users early.

03 · DECIDE

The decision

Name the tension, pick a side, and be able to defend that choice to the team.

04 · MEASURE

The outcome

Check what the decision produces in the real product, and remove what doesn't help.
